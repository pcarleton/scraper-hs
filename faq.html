FAQ

Who made this?
Hi, I'm Paul, and I put this together. If you liked it, let me know on Twitter!
Where do I find my Venmo username?
In the mobile app, touch the settings button in the top left corner. Your username will be displayed with a leading "@" sign.
Are you affiliated with Venmo?
No.
Where does this data come from?
Venmo's public profile pages. You can see yours by going to venmo.com/[username]. This page shows the last 5 Public transactions for a user (as opposed to Friends-Only or Private).
How can I stop this data from being public?
You can change your default privacy settings from the Venmo account page.
